The first-ever festival will bring fans together for two days of performances across two stages with no overlapping sets between the 20 artists. No schedule conflicts on this river bank! Check out all the festival has to offer with premium ticket options. Enjoy historic riverboat rides, comfortable lounges, exclusive food options, air-conditioned restrooms, and more! The Minnesota Yacht Club Festival will take place at Harriet Island Regional Park in the heart of downtown St. Paul, Minnesota. Experience sound waves underneath the giant cottonwoods & along the majestic Mississippi River.
